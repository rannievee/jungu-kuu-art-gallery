OUR HISTORY

Jungu Kuu Art Gallery was opened on 1st January 2018 by a group of three artistic friends who had dedicated their life to art and expertised on it. It has grown since through mergers and partnerships to the point we are now. We started by exhibiting paintings and it worked very well with a big sale. Then we started doing beadwork and then after we used the profits to open a studio and our photography is now widely loved.We do the photography in weddings, graduations and other occassions.

OUR MISSION

To ensure art grows great both in the business and creative world by creating exhibitions for artists.

OUR VISION

Make art the greatest expressionist in both the business world and social world.
